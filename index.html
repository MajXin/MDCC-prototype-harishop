About MDCC

The mission of a college cultural club focused on Dogri language is to promote and preserve the rich cultural heritage of Dogri-speaking regions. Through various activities such as language workshops, cultural performances, and awareness campaigns, the club aims to foster a deeper appreciation for Dogri language, literature, and traditions among students and the broader community. It also seeks to create a platform for dialogue and celebration of Dogri culture, strengthening cultural identity and unity.

About MIET

Model Institute of Engineering and Technology (MIET), established in 1996, holds the distinction of being the first private engineering college in Jammu and Kashmir, setting a precedent for private education in the region. As a part of the esteemed MIER Group, which has an impressive legacy spanning over 85 years, MIET stands as a symbol of excellence and innovation in the field of higher education.

MIET is not only a leader in the realm of engineering but also a champion in fostering technological talent. We are proud to be the first institution to establish an IEEE Student Branch in Jammu, which has since grown to become the largest Student Branch in the entire Jammu region.
