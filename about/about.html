Benifits for IEEE members

Technical Knowledge and Resources

IEEE provides access to a wealth of technical knowledge, research papers, journals, and publications, helping members stay updated with the latest advancements in their fields.

Networking

IEEE offers a vast network of professionals, which can be highly beneficial for students and experienced engineers alike. It provides opportunities to connect with experts, mentors, and peers.

Career Development

IEEE offers resources for career development, including job listings, resume-building tools, and career advice. It can be a valuable asset for those looking to advance their careers in technology-related fields.

Conferences and Events

IEEE hosts numerous conferences, symposiums, and events, where members can present their research, learn fromothers, and collaborate on projects.

Industry Insights

IEEE provides access to industry trends, market analysis, and reports, allowing members to stay informed about the latest developments and opportunities.

Scholarships and Awards

IEEE offers scholarships and awards to recognize outstanding contributionsto the field of technology and engineering.

IEEE SB MIET Global View

IEEE MIET Student Branch is one of the largest student branches in the entire Jammu region. The IEEE SB MIET is governed by the IEEE Delhi Section and the Chandigarh Sub-Section.

IEEE Delhi Section IEEE Delhi Section is one of the 13 Sections in India Council coming under Asia-Pacific Region, the Region-10 of IEEE. At present it covers entire northern part of the country consisting of the four states of Rajasthan, Haryana, Punjab, Himachal Pradesh, and the National Capital Territory of Delhi, Union Territories of Chandigarh, Jammu & Kashmir, Ladakh.

IEEE Chandigarh Sub-Section The Chandigarh Subsection of IEEE Delhi Section was formed with Dr. Chepuri S. Rao, SMIEEE as its Interim Chair on June 18, 2005. Subsequently with him as regular Chairperson Executive Committee of Subsection was constituted.
